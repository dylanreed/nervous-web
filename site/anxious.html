Community Intelligence That Actually Matters

Go beyond vanity metrics. Anxious Analytics uses AI to understand what's really happening in your community—sentiment, relationships, health, and risk—so you can make decisions based on insight, not guesswork.

The Problem

Current Discord analytics tools miss what matters:

  • Word clouds tell you nothing about how people actually feel
  • Message counts reward spam, not quality conversation
  • Emoji-heavy engagement scores are meaningless
  • No way to see who's actually building relationships vs just posting
  • Moderators flying blind without data-driven priorities
  • Problem members identified after damage is done, not before

The Solution

Anxious Analytics brings real intelligence to community management with AI that understands context, not just counts.

GPT-4 Sentiment Analysis

Forget word clouds. Our sentiment analysis uses GPT-4 to understand the actual emotional tone of conversations—sarcasm, frustration, enthusiasm, and everything in between. Know how your community really feels.

Wilson Confidence Scoring

We use the Wilson Confidence Index—the same algorithm Reddit and Amazon use for rankings—to measure conversation quality. This filters out low-quality interactions like emoji spam and surfaces genuinely valuable discussions.

User Relationship Mapping

See how your community actually connects. Our relationship scoring analyzes:

  • Response patterns between users
  • Tagging and mention behavior
  • Sequential messaging that forms real conversations
  • Confidence ratings (85%+ for quality interactions)

Visual conversation flow shows you the top contributors and how they interact over time.

Community Health Scoring

Get an overall health score for your community with AI-generated actionable insights:

  • "20 out of 22 channels have no messages—lack of engagement"
  • User-specific recommendations based on influence and helpfulness
  • Automated role suggestions (community champion, moderator, ambassador)
  • Retention strategies for low-engagement members

Automated Moderation Intelligence

Daily Moderator Checklists

Each moderator gets personalized daily tasks based on server analytics—not generic to-dos, but specific actions with priority levels (high/medium/low) across conversation, engagement, and sentiment categories.

Built-in Accountability

  • Timer system with webhook reminders every 30 minutes
  • Auto mod log checks and welcome channel sweeps
  • Support ticket monitoring
  • Performance tracking for all moderator actions

Member Risk Assessment

Identify problems before they happen with our "Minority Report" approach:

  • Leverages Discord's hidden spam flags (1-20 scale)
  • Proactive identification of potential bad actors
  • Timeout tracking with reason logging and moderator attribution
  • Integration with WIC global ban database for preemptive filtering

Built Right

  • Real-time processing — Handles 400K+ member servers
  • GDPR compliant — On-demand data processing, not persistent storage
  • Privacy focused — Working toward private LLM infrastructure
  • Multi-tenant ready — Easy deployment across multiple servers

Best For

  • Large communities needing data-driven decisions
  • Brands tracking community sentiment
  • Mod teams wanting accountability and priorities
  • Community managers measuring real engagement
  • Anyone tired of meaningless vanity metrics
