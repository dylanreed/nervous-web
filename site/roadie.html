QR-Powered Discord Onboarding for Live Events

The Problem

Live events create incredible energy, but that momentum disappears when attendees go home:

  • Asking attendees to manually search for your Discord server loses most of them
  • No way to recognize event attendees once they join your community
  • The excitement and connection from in-person events fades quickly
  • Event staff are too busy to manually onboard community members
  • Past attendees are your best audience for future events, but you can't reach them

The Solution

Roadie bridges the gap between your live events and your online community with frictionless onboarding.

Instant QR Onboarding

No more asking attendees to search for your server or type in addresses. Simply display a QR code at your event—on screens, posters, badges, or handouts—and attendees scan to instantly join your Discord.

Automatic Role Assignment

Every attendee who joins through Roadie automatically gets a special role—"Summer Tour 2025," "VIP," "EventCon Attendee," "Early Supporter"—making them feel recognized and valued from the moment they arrive.

Exclusive Access

Use event-specific roles to unlock:

  • VIP channels and private areas
  • Post-event Q&As with artists, speakers, or team members
  • Event-specific promotions and giveaways
  • Early access to future event tickets

Extend Your Event's Lifespan

A concert lasts a few hours. A conference lasts a few days. But the community you build through Roadie keeps the discussions, networking, and brand engagement going long after everyone has gone home.

Fully Automated

Your event staff doesn't need to manually add users or assign roles. Roadie handles the entire process, letting your team focus on delivering an amazing event experience.
