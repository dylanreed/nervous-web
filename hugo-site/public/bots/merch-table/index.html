Merch Table

Turn your community into customers.

Connect your community to your Shopify store.

Merch Table lets you offer exclusive products to members based on their Discord roles — Server Boosters, Patreon supporters, or any custom role you create. Perfect for creators, gaming communities, and anyone who wants to reward their most engaged Discord members with exclusive merch.

The Problem

Running exclusive merch for your Discord community is a hassle:

  • No way to verify Discord roles on your Shopify store
  • Manual verification doesn't scale
  • Exclusive products get shared with non-members
  • Role changes don't sync to store access
  • No connection between your community and commerce

The Solution

Merch Table bridges your Discord server and Shopify store. Members link their accounts once, and their Discord roles automatically control what they can purchase.

Role-Based Product Access

Restrict any product to specific Discord roles. Only members with the right roles can see and purchase exclusive items.

Automatic Sync

When a member gains or loses a role in Discord, their Shopify customer tags update automatically. No manual intervention required.

Cart Validation

Even if someone finds a direct link to a restricted product, Merch Table validates their roles at checkout. No unauthorized purchases slip through.

Simple Account Linking

Members connect their Discord and Shopify accounts with a single slash command: /link_shopify. The bot handles the OAuth flow and links the accounts securely.

Admin Dashboard

Manage visibility rules, monitor linked accounts, and troubleshoot issues from a clean admin interface.

All Commands

Member Commands

/link_shopify /unlink_shopify /shopify_status

Admin Commands

/merchtable config /merchtable rules add /merchtable rules remove /merchtable rules list

Troubleshooting

/merchtable lookup /merchtable sync

Built Right

  • Secure OAuth — Industry-standard authentication for both Discord and Shopify
  • Real-time sync — Role changes reflect immediately
  • Shopify native — Works with your existing store setup
  • Privacy-focused — Only stores the minimum data needed

Best For

  • Server Boosters — Exclusive merch for members who boost your server
  • Patreon Supporters — Sync Patreon roles to unlock special products
  • VIP Members — Reward your most active community members
  • Early Access — Let specific roles purchase new drops first
  • Limited Editions — Restrict rare items to long-time members

Get Started

Set up Merch Table in minutes:

  1. Install Merch Table on your Shopify store
  2. Connect your Discord server
  3. Set up visibility rules (which roles can see which products)
  4. Members link their accounts with /link_shopify in Discord
  5. Linked members with the right roles can purchase exclusive products
