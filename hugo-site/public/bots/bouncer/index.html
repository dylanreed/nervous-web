The Problem

Managing a growing Discord server is chaos:

  • Mods forget why someone was warned three months ago
  • Bulk role updates go wrong with no way to undo them
  • Raiders slip through because you can't pre-ban by ID
  • Discord's 28-day timeout limit isn't enough for serious cases
  • No visibility into which mods are active or what rules get broken most

The Solution

bouncer is a moderation powerhouse designed for servers that need more than basic tools.

Flexible Discipline

Timeouts that work your way—30 minutes, 7 days, or indefinite. Go beyond Discord's 28-day limit when you need to. Quarantine isolates problem members by removing all roles and assigning a quarantine role. When it's over, their original roles are automatically restored. No spreadsheets required. Warnings link directly to your server rules. Track which rules get broken and how often.

Complete History

Every action is logged. Every action can be searched. /modhistory @user shows you everything—warnings, timeouts, bans, the works—with pagination for long histories. Made a mistake? /reverse undoes it and logs that too.

Bulk Operations Without Fear

Need to add a role to everyone who joined after a certain date? /bulkrole handles it. Made an error? Rollback within 24 hours. Full operation history included.

Raid Protection

/preban lets you ban known troublemakers by user ID before they ever join. /watchlist monitors suspicious members and alerts you when they leave.

Actionable Reports

/modreport generates analytics: actions by type, top moderators by activity, most moderated users, and rule violation breakdown. Export to CSV for external analysis or share the embed in your staff channel.

Built Right

  • Permission-aware — Can't moderate users with equal or higher roles
  • Owner protected — Server owners can never be moderated
  • Auto-expiry — Timeouts and quarantines end on schedule
  • Audit-ready — Every action logged with moderator, reason, and timestamp

Best For

  • Large gaming communities
  • Creator and streamer servers
  • Educational Discord servers
  • Professional communities needing compliance
  • Any server with a moderation team

Get Started

Add bouncer to your server and run:

  1. /config log-channel #mod-logs — Set your logging channel
  2. /config quarantine-role @Quarantine — Set your quarantine role
  3. Start moderating with confidence
