General Questions

Google Gemini Discord Server

What is its purpose?

The community provides a constructive feedback source for their developer team, allowing them to interact with users on a more personal level to better assess consumer sentiment and needs surrounding the product. Free from the distraction of most social channels, community members are able to give more thoughtful, honest feedback, and connect with members of their development team in a smaller and more intimate setting.

What nervous delivered

nervous worked with their brand engagement team to create a private Discord server with gated enrollment and user verification systems. nervous served as a direct liaison between Discord and the client, helping the client understand server culture and how best to use the platform to accomplish their messaging and feedback gathering goals.

David Choe's Munko

What is its purpose?

Client and their team wanted to create an evolving membership card token that reflected the changing nature of their art and granted the holder access to a unique, experiential community centered around their art, media appearances, and personal spiritual journey.

What nervous delivered

nervous set up a custom Discord server and crafted a “make them wait” strategy, launching with only one channel, no explanation, and a moderation team who became part of the overall storytelling.

Less Than Jake

What is its purpose?

Create a membership-based fan club and online community that allowed the band to connect with fans, offer fun exclusives, and deliver demo tracks, merch discounts, and other insider info.

What nervous delivered

nervous created a Discord server that served as the home base for the first official fan club for the 30-year old band. nervous handled all server architecture and ongoing moderation and community management.

We then worked with the creator of the bands iconic mascot to develop a unique collection of membership tokens that featured imagery from their entire discography and history.
