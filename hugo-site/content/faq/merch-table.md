questions:
  - q: "What is merch table?"
    a: "merch table connects your Discord server to your Shopify store, letting you offer exclusive products to members based on their Discord roles."
  - q: "How do members link their accounts?"
    a: "Members use the <code>/link_shopify</code> command in Discord. They'll receive a unique link to connect their Shopify customer account to their Discord profile."
  - q: "What happens when someone loses a role?"
    a: "merch table automatically syncs role changes. When a member loses a role, their Shopify customer tags update and they lose access to role-restricted products."
  - q: "Can someone buy a restricted product if they remove it from their cart after losing a role?"
    a: "No. merch table validates cart contents at checkout. Even if a product was added while they had the role, the purchase will be blocked if they no longer qualify."
